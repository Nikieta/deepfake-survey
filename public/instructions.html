Instructions for the Listening Task

Please read carefully before starting the survey

What you will do in this study

  • You will listen to short audio clips, each lasting approximately 10 to 20 seconds.
  • Each clip may contain a specific audio characteristic or artifact.
  • After listening, you will answer a few questions about what you perceive.

Please follow these guidelines for best results

  • Use headphones or earphones, recommended.
  • Sit in a quiet environment.
  • Adjust your volume to a comfortable level before starting.
  • You may replay each audio clip if needed.
  • There are no right or wrong answers, please answer based on what you hear.
  • If you are unsure, choose the option that best matches your perception.
  • Please listen carefully and answer thoughtfully. Responses submitted without listening may affect the quality of the study.
